🫀 VitalHealth

AI-Powered Physiological Digital Twin


A full-stack open-source platform coupling a React Native app with a BioGears physiological simulation backend.

Tech Stack: React Native Expo, FastAPI, Python, BioGears (C++)

The Problem with Current Health Apps

Typical Apps

  • Data Storage: They just log what you did.
  • Models: Rely on simple statistical estimates.
  • Vitals: Only use external sensor readings.

Our Approach

  • Simulation: Simulates the true physiological response.
  • Models: Mechanistic organ-level simulation.
  • Vitals: BioGears engine (creates a continuous snapshot).

VitalHealth tracks what happens inside your body as a result.

The Power of BioGears

BioGears Engine is a peer-reviewed, open-source C++ human physiology simulator.

  • Creates a living computational model (a Digital Twin) of your body.
  • When you log a meal, workout, or sleep — it runs a real physiological simulation.
  • Computes second-by-second changes in:
    • Heart Rate & Blood Pressure
    • Blood Glucose & Insulin
    • Oxygen Saturation (SpO₂)
    • Core Temp & Cardiac Output

AI Health Chat (Privacy-First)

An On-Device RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) pipeline.

  1. Upload your medical records (PDFs & images).
  2. On-Device: Text is extracted via OCR and intelligently chunked.
  3. On-Device: Chunks are embedded and stored locally.
  4. When you chat, only relevant pieces are sent to a local LLM, guaranteeing 100% Privacy.

Symptom Logging: Dynamic flow with AI-generated follow-ups.
